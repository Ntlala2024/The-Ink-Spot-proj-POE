Our Story

The Ink Spot Printing and Ads was established by passionate professionals with over 4 years of experience in the printing industry. Based in the Turfloop Mankweng Polokwane shopping complex, we serve the entire worldwide.

We recognized a tremendous opportunity in Turfloop to provide specialized and personalized copying and printing services that meet the unique that lure our customers and needs of our community.

Our vision is to provide excellent client care, quality service and customer support while impacting the economy of South Africa through job opportunities and people empowerment.

What sets us apart is our commitment to customer excellence, excellent customer relations, and fast service delivery.
